About CEA Industries

CEA Industries is a publicly traded company committed to creating long-term shareholder value through strategic digital asset management and operational excellence.

Company Overview

Our Mission

To create sustainable long-term value for shareholders through disciplined capital allocation, strategic digital asset management, and transparent governance.

Business Model

CEA Industries operates as a holding company with a primary focus on digital asset treasury management through strategic BNB acquisitions, alongside legacy business operations.

Governance & Transparency

We maintain the highest standards of corporate governance, with regular disclosure, independent board oversight, and comprehensive investor communications.

Shareholder Focus

Every strategic decision is made with shareholder value in mind, balancing growth opportunities with prudent risk management and capital discipline.

Digital Asset Treasury Strategy

CEA Industries has adopted a disciplined approach to digital asset management, focusing on BNB as a strategic treasury asset. This strategy allows our shareholders to gain indirect exposure to the BNB ecosystem while benefiting from our active management and governance structure.

Our treasury operations are characterized by:

  • • Disciplined acquisition strategy with transparent cost basis tracking
  • • Periodically updated dashboard reporting of holdings and performance metrics
  • • Long-term hold philosophy aligned with shareholder value creation
  • • Regular disclosure and comprehensive investor communications
