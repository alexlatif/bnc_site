Our Businesses

CEA Industries operates through a focused digital asset treasury strategy and legacy business operations, all aimed at creating long-term shareholder value.

BNB Treasury

Our core strategic focus is the disciplined acquisition and management of BNB digital assets. Through our treasury operations, CEA Industries provides shareholders with indirect exposure to the BNB ecosystem, combining the benefits of digital asset holdings with the governance and transparency of a public company structure.

The BNB Treasury strategy is built on transparent reporting, disciplined capital allocation, and a long-term value creation philosophy. All holdings, performance metrics, and key statistics are available in real-time through our comprehensive dashboard.
